Direction for:

LEMON SODA

Lemonsoda is a brand of Italian carbonated soft drinks created in the 1940s.

The original flavor of Lemonsoda was lemon. Oransoda and Pelmosoda, orange and grapefruit flavored soft drinks, were introduced in subsequent years.

The lemon soda debuts new flavors with an energy drink.
