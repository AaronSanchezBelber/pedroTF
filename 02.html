Creative Direction & Direction for:

ECLIPSE / KARONT

Through this piece for Karont's OutKast collection, we follow the emotional journey of a man as he confronts his fears and difficult moments to ultimately find relief.

Immersed in an inner journey, he faces his fears and overcomes obstacles, using virtual production to create immersive landscapes and sets that enhance the narrative of the piece.
