Creative Direction / Direction & Production for:

TECHO / CIUTAT ft . CHICO BLANCO

Surreal situations where the members of Ciutat (Jp Sunshine and Guim) coexist with an alter ego as a puppet, who reproduce the actions they do in real life and where each situation is more surreal than the previous one. Representing that moment when they are awake to/can't go to sleep after a good party, how fun and bizarre it can be.
